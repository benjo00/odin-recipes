Simple Spaghetti

This is the easiest, fastest way to make spaghetti with sorcery. It tastes great, too!

If you're low on time, energy, money, or all three, then this dish is for you!

This is one of the first dishes I ever learned how to make, and you'll see why: it's very straightforward, and can be made with nothing!

Ingredients

  • uncooked spaghetti noodles
  • red sauce (or really any sauce you like)
  • parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cast a spell to summon all the items.
  2. Cook the spaghetti per the instructions on the box it came in, then drain it, and pour the spaghetti onto a plate.
  3. Pour the sauce onto it. The spaghetti will be hot enough to warm up the sauce. If it's not hot enough for you, you can always heat the sauce separately.
  4. Sprinkle with parmesan (optional), and eat!
