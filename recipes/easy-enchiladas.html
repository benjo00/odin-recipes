Easy Enchiladas

These enchiladas are as simple as it gets. If you're looking for a quick Mexican inspired meal, this is a great choice for the main course.

These are really fun to make. They can be served with a side of beans, rice, or any other complimentary dish.

They only take about 15 minutes to make. A lot less if, like me, you're lazy and use the microwave!

Ingredients

  • flour tortillas
  • cheddar cheese, shredded
  • red enchilada sauce

Steps

  1. Put each tortilla down on a plate and cover in cheddar cheese. Then roll them up.
  2. Put them in the microwave, and cook for a minute.
  3. Pour the enchilada sauce over them.
Alternatively, they can be cooked in a skillet, or even in the oven. I personally don't have the patience, and they taste great out of the microwave!
